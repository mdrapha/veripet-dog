# VeriPet Master Presentation

English Beamer presentation for the PPG-CC / UNIFESP Artificial Intelligence course.

## Contents

- `main.tex`: Beamer source based on the original TCC presentation style.
- `figures/`: copied figures required by the slides.

## Scope

The presentation follows the research article structure:

- motivation;
- research gaps;
- related work and state of the art;
- methodology;
- datasets and experimental protocol;
- breed classification results;
- individual verification results;
- breed-based fusion analysis;
- scientific impact;
- reproducibility.

## Compilation

Use pdfLaTeX:

```bash
pdflatex main.tex
pdflatex main.tex
```
